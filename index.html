My name is Aubrey Mark and I was born and raised in Salt Lake County, Utah. I have a passion for baking. That started when I was little, baking holiday goodies with my grandma, which I still love to do. A lot of traditional Italian cookies and breads. In my 20's I started to play around with fondont and gum paste and watching a lot of Food Network, from there my cakes really started to evolve. People kept telling me I should open a bakery, but that costs a lot of money, so the next best thing, I decided to start a side business, created a name, Charming Sweets, and word of mouth just got people calling me for cakes. I currently work for a Company called Lendio,we help small business across the nation find funding. I am also attending the University of Utah Coding Boot Camp to try and better my career.
